ABOUT CRUS

CRUS Esports was founded in 2022 with a vision to compete at the highest level of Mobile Legends: Bang Bang professional gaming. Our team consists of five dedicated players who have trained extensively to master their respective roles and work together as a cohesive unit.

We believe in the power of teamwork, strategic thinking, and continuous improvement. Our training regimen includes daily practice sessions, strategy discussions, and performance analysis to ensure we stay competitive in the ever-evolving landscape of Mobile Legends esports.

Our goal is not just to win tournaments, but to inspire the next generation of esports athletes and contribute to the growth of the Mobile Legends community worldwide.

Our Mission

To dominate the Mobile Legends: Bang Bang competitive scene through dedication, teamwork, and strategic excellence while maintaining the highest standards of sportsmanship and professionalism.
